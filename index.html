How To Add A New Task

Fake Image

Look under the "To Do Calendar" and click on the "Add New Task" button. You can either select a pre-entered task from the drop down list or go to other and type in your own task. You will also need to set a date for twhem the task is due. You also have the optiuon of being more precise and adding in a time the task needs to be finished. Then you will choose either, "Low", "Medium" or a "High" priority for your task; then submit your form. After filling out and submitting your new task, click on the "X" in the top right corner to return to the homepage.

*Now that you have a task submitted, you will be able to to see your task on the To Do Calendar signified by the task title and color of the corresponding priority choice..
